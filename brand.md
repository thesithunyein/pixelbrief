# PixelBrief brand

## Mark
Black rounded plate (`#1D1D1F`) + one Apple-blue pixel (`#0071E3`) + two brief bars (white).  
Meaning: a creative brief, condensed to a pixel.

## Wordmark
**PixelBrief** (one word). Display: Instrument Serif. UI: Manrope.
